why

Some of my friends have convinced me to start writing online in medium-long form.

There are some great online writers of which I am not near; I am quite poor at decoding my inner thoughts from latents to prose. Most of my writing will probably contain sentences that only appear plausible from an angle.

Mainly I'll be writing about machine learning, robotics, and computational physics. Occassionally I may write about things in aerospace I find interesting. Anyways, the main point of this page is to a. describe my internal representations of interesting science publicly, and b. get better at writing.

My journal is extremely large and there are definitely good parts of it, so most of my writing will sample excerpts of myself in the past with residual additions of how my opinion has changed since. Knowing how I am, it is extremely likely I edit/rewrite old posts over time. Like when life of pablo came out and there were 40 different versions you could pirate.
-rishi
